# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming and respectful community. This project adopts the spirit of the Contributor Covenant (v2.1).

## Our Standards
- Be respectful, inclusive, and constructive.
- Focus on the technical problem; assume good intent.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Enforcement
Reports can be sent privately as described in `SECURITY.md`. Maintainers will review and act as appropriate.
